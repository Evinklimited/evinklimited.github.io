About

Evin K Limited

Evin K Limited is a UK-based company, incorporated in England and Wales in 2016, and focused on renewable-energy development, energy-sector assessment and early-stage strategic project advancement.

The company’s current positioning is centred on feasibility-stage renewable-energy development, electricity-generation potential, grid-related evaluation and broader electricity-sector opportunity assessment within Wales and the wider United Kingdom.

Evin K Limited recognises the increasing importance of resilient renewable-electricity generation, electricity-network resilience and responsible energy development within the United Kingdom’s evolving energy-transition pathway.

The company’s broader development interests include participation in renewable-electricity generation and associated electricity-sector development within Wales and the wider United Kingdom.
